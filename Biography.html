Yohannes solomon

I am yohannes solomon. I was born on july 3 1991, in muger and my parents are solomon fikru and meserete fekadu. I have two young siblings. I am the eldest in my family. I attend kinder garten upto eighth reji primary school. While there i participated in football club. From grade ninth upto 12th i attended at muger community secondary and preparatory school. That time was so hard for me because i had to go long distance from school to home and from home to school. I devoted myself to study hard which helped me to pass university entrance ecamination with good grade. After schoring good grade I joined addis ababa university which i am proud of a lot. I am currently learning in Ethiopian institute of architecture and construction. I am striving to achieve a good grade and graduate with distinciton. I serve my community with knowledge and skill i have got.
